What we do

Organizations can choose to outsource a complete software development project, including its project control. Another option is to augment existing project teams with temporary craftsmen. In this staff augmentation model the customer also takes ownership on project control. We support both service models and comply to the Agile Scrum collaboration framework. Delivery with great speed, with no concessions to quality, is one of our first priorities. In The Netherlands always at least one (Dutch speaking) lead developer is present to support customers with both technical implementation and requirement engineering.

Outsourcing

Your focus is developing and growing your business. The IT governance level of your company is such that software development can be completely outsourced. Sosaro is your logical and cost efficient delivery partner. We take full ownership of all your software development projects, including project control and Agile Scrum collaboration. A model perfectly suitable for delivery of mobile and web e-commerce solutions. Based on our experience as well as our technology stack.



IT Staff Augmentation

Project teams can have a need for staff augmentation based on the lack of specific skills or growth of number of tasks. For strategic or tactical reasons the project governance remains with the customer. Sosaro craftsmen can now be added to existing project teams based on an IT Staff Augmentation agreement. Timely addition of requested professionals is crucial to us then. Both onsite in The Netherlands and remote in Indonesia engineers can be allocated. We deliver within short lead times based on our candidate database & smart recruitment channels. As a special service, we can help startups staffing their complete technical team.
